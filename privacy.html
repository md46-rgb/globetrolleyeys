Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect name, email, shipping address, and payment information (processed securely by Stripe/PayPal). We never store raw card data. We also collect anonymous analytics via Google Analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill orders
  • To send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • To improve our website experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. No Local Database Storage

We do not store payment card data on our servers. All payment information is handled directly by Stripe or PayPal using token-based secure handling.

4. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

You have the right to access, correct, delete your data, and opt out of marketing. Contact: privacy@globetrolleyeys.store
